Chicken Caesar Wrap

Description

Quick, Easy and Healthy, this Chicken Caesar Wrap recipe is perfect for breakfast lunch or dinner and can be very easily modified or upgraded to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Croutons
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Caesar Salad Dressing
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Toss Ingredients: Add chicken, croutons, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and Caesar salad dressing to a bowl and toss well to combine
  2. Fill Tortilla and Roll: Add a big spoonful of mixture to a tortillaa dn roll tightly, folding sides in like a burrito. Secure with toothpicks if necessary.
